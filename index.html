🧠 Tom Goodwin's Core Lessons
Most businesses "digitize the past" — they replicate old processes digitally rather than questioning whether those processes should exist at all.
A digital layer on a broken foundation is still broken. True transformation requires rethinking the core business model, not just adding an app or portal.
The mindset shift: technology is not a tool for efficiency — it is a lens through which to completely reimagine customer value creation.
The most powerful technologies disappear. When AI is truly transformative, users won't think about "using AI" — it's just how things work.
